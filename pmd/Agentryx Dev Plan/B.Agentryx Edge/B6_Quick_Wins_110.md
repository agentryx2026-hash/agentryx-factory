## 1. The 110 Labs Analysis

### How This Works
After analyzing the project scope (A1-A3), Troi scans for low-effort / high-delight features that the customer **didn't ask for** but will be thrilled to receive.

### Selection Criteria
Only Quick Wins that meet ALL of these are included:
- ⏱️ **Effort:** Less than 1 agent-cycle to implement
- 🎯 **Impact:** Noticeable improvement to user experience
- 🔒 **Risk:** Zero risk to core functionality
- 📦 **Self-Contained:** No new dependencies or infrastructure
